Case Study: GreenTech Innovations

When GreenTech Innovations approached us, they were struggling to communicate the benefits of their solar energy solutions to potential customers. Their website had outdated content, poor search rankings, and minimal social media presence.

Our comprehensive approach included:

  1. Complete website redesign with user-friendly navigation
  2. SEO optimization targeting relevant keywords in the renewable energy sector
  3. Content marketing strategy featuring educational blog posts and case studies
  4. Social media campaigns highlighting customer success stories
  5. Pay-per-click advertising targeting homeowners interested in solar solutions

Within six months, GreenTech saw a 340% increase in website traffic, a 280% improvement in lead generation, and a 190% boost in qualified sales inquiries.
